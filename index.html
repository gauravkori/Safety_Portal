SAFETY TIPS FOR GIRLS

Be Aware of Your Surroundings:

  • Stay alert and be aware of what's happening around you.
  • Avoid distractions like excessive use of headphones or staring at your phone while walking in unfamiliar areas.

Trust Your Instincts:

  • If something feels off or uncomfortable, trust your gut feelings and take action accordingly.
  • If you feel uneasy about a person or a situation, remove yourself from it.

Stay in Well-Lit Areas:

  • Stick to well-lit paths and streets, especially at night.
  • Avoid shortcuts through dark alleys or secluded areas.

Inform Someone About Your Plans:

  • Let someone know where you are going and when you expect to return.
  • Share your plans with friends or family members, especially if you are going to a new or unfamiliar place.

Use Reliable Transportation:

  • When using public transportation, choose well-lit and busy stops.
  • If using rideshare services, verify the driver's information before getting into the vehicle.

Have Emergency Contacts:

  • Keep important contacts programmed into your phone.
  • Memorize emergency numbers, and consider having a list of contacts readily accessible in case your phone is unavailable.
